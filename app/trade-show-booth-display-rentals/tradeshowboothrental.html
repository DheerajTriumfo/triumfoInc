Got questions? We’ve got answers. Explore our FAQs to quickly find insights about our trade show services, processes, and solutions.

Should I rent or buy a trade show booth?

Renting is ideal for occasional shows; buying is better for frequent exhibitors seeking consistent branding.

How much does a trade show booth cost?

The cost varies based on size, materials, and customization. Smaller rentals start at a few thousand dollars, while large custom booths with lighting, graphics, and double decks can go much higher.

How long does it take to build a booth?

Rental booths can be ready within 1–3 weeks, while custom-built designs typically require 6–12 weeks for design, approval, and fabrication.

What’s included in your booth services?

We provide complete end-to-end services — including design, fabrication, printing, logistics, installation, dismantling, and storage — all handled by our in-house team.

Do you handle booth setup and dismantling?

Yes, our skilled team manages everything from on-site installation to dismantling after the show, ensuring a smooth, hassle-free experience for you.
